Telecom & Cloud

Be it classic Telecom products like SMS/MMS gateways, load balancers, Diameter Routing Agents, or Cloud Messaging Architectures: We have the skills and experience to design, deliver and operate great Telecom and Cloud products.

IoT & M2M

Your Internet of Things (IoT) and Machine to Machine (M2M) projects are going to need a solid server backend application or broker architecture. Are you aware of the best protocols and practices? Let's discuss your ideas and needs.

Smart Grid & Smart City

If your Smart Grid communication is based on a packet switched network or is connected to the internet, it is going to need Smart Routing Middleware and Policy Enforcement points. So does your city, if you want it to be smart and sustainable.
